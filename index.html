Simple Kimchi Ramyun Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. It's spicy, tasty and super satisfying.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Korean instant ramen (recommended Jin Kimchi)
  • Kimchi
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion
  • Korean chili flakes (optional)
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Make the base: Add 3/4 of the seasoning powder packet in boiling water and add chopped kimchi.
  2. Boil noodles: Add the ramen noodles, dried vegetable flakes and soft boiled eggs to top off the bowl of ramen. Let them cook for 3-4 minutes, or until they reach your preferred structure. Optionally, sprinkle in some Korean chili flakes.
  3. Serve and garnish: Once done, turn off the heat. Add green onion and drizzle with sesame oil. Serve right away and enjoy your delicious Kimchi Ramen.
